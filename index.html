Instrucions:

1. Position your pointing device (e.g. mouse) in the Red rectangle.

2. With one hand click the Space Key in your keyboard

4. With the same hand click as fast as possible the other rectangle using your pointing device.

3. Repeat three times

How it works:

Fitt's Law Formula:

Movment Time = A + B * log2 (D/S + c)

Where:

A = ime to adjust or grasp to the device and the time to make the selection or click. (Pointing Device Dependant - Measured in sec/bits)

B = Eye Hand coordination with the device. (Pointing Device Dependant - Measured in sec/bits)

bits = rate of human information processing

-- "Harder movements result in a higher count of bits" --

D = Distance from the cursor's start to the middle of the target (Measured in Pixels or Centimiters)

S = Width of the targer (Measured in Pixels or Centimiters)

c = A constant of 0, 0.5 or 1

Mouse Movment Time Model:

Movment Time = 1.03 + 0.1 * log2 (D/S + 0.5)

Joystick Movment Time Model:

Movment Time = 0.99 + 0.22 * log2 (D/S + 0.5)
